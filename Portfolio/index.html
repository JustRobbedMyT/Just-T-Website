Service Technician

No two days are the same in Customer Service and Support. I have had the privilege of solving challenging and complex problems every day, collaborating and sharing knowledge with others and building solutions to enable customers to make the most of software and services.

Website Design

I have had the pleasure of working on a number of web-based projects. During the fast- paced process of building out websites, I have honed my skills in organization and design. Website construction has also helped me build my coding skills up and given me an outlet for my creativity.

Swift Development

Working in the swift programming language began as a curiosity for me. I had wanted to learn how to create a small application to show off on my phone. Now, after building my first simple recipe app, I have come to enjoy working within the interface of x-code and have been looking forward to building out other projects in this space.
